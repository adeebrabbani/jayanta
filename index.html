About Adv. Jayanta Kumar Chakraborty

For over 10 years, I have been dedicated to helping individuals and businesses navigate complex legal challenges in Belgharia, Nimta, Birati, Barrackpore & Barasat. My approach is centered on providing clear, strategic, and effective representation to protect your interests.

I understand that legal matters can be stressful and overwhelming. That's why I prioritize clear communication, keeping you informed at every step, and providing practical solutions tailored to your specific situation.

Questions people ask before they book

Is the first consultation free? +

The first consult is paid. After that, follow-up consults can be free depending on your matter. You'll receive a written estimate with no hidden charges.

Do you take phone calls or only in person? +

I prefer in-chamber, face-to-face meetings to truly understand you. For quick questions and updates, I respond on WhatsApp immediately.

Which cases and courts do you handle? +

Individuals in family, property, criminal, and civil matters. I appear in Barasat, Barrackpore and Bidhannagar courts , and at the Calcutta High Court when needed.
