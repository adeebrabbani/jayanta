Questions people ask before they book

Is the first consultation free? +

The first consult is paid. After that, follow-up consults can be free depending on your matter. You'll receive a written estimate with no hidden charges.

Do you take phone calls or only in person? +

I prefer in-chamber, face-to-face meetings to truly understand you. For quick questions and updates, I respond on WhatsApp immediately.

Which cases and courts do you handle? +

Individuals in family, property, criminal, and civil matters. I appear in Barasat, Bidhannagar, and Alipore District Courts, and at the Calcutta High Court when needed.
